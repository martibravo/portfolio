Complete visual identity for a small architecture practice. The brief called for something austere and enduring — nothing fashionable.

<Figure src="/images/placeholder.svg" alt="Primary logotype" caption="Primary logotype, two weights" />

The wordmark is set in a modified version of Akzidenz Grotesk. Letter-spacing increased by 12%. The second weight was used exclusively for the address block on print materials.

## Print

A4 letterhead, compliment slip, and envelope. Black on off-white stock. No color. No texture.
